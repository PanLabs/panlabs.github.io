Tundra

iPhones are a funny thing. With great cameras that take extremely high-quality images, an iPhone is loved until you see the gut-wrenching “You’re out of storage” notification. That’s why we helped develop, design and release Tundra. Tundra is an iOS app that allows users to easily take photos without using/losing any of the storage on the device. The app allows people to care more about capturing the shot than what they have to delete to take another picture.

  • Category: iOS Development, Application Design, App Publishing

ROOK

As our smart devices get smaller and smaller, it feels as if the storage constraints only get larger and larger. In a world where data & information are critical elements of our daily lives, what if there was a way to seamless expand the storage of all of your devices with one simple piece of hardware.

Enter Rook: The pocket-sized device that you can grab and go to easily expand the memory of all of your devices.

  • Category: iOS Development, Application Design, Hardware Design, Product Launch Strategies

Foodspace

What is Foodspace? Almost exactly what it sounds like: a space for you to manage your recipes, meal plans, ingredients and weekly shopping list. The goal? Make it easy for roommates, spouses, families, etc. to coordinate the preparation of meals and cut down the frustration of shopping in households.

  • Category: iOS Development, Application Design, App Publishing
